Something got lost between then and now.

Do you remember the summer holidays when you were a child? The stories told under the mango tree. The games nobody taught you—somebody's older cousin just showed you one afternoon and suddenly the whole street was playing.

Children today grow up with screens but less connection to stories, creativity, and culture. We believe screens are not the enemy, but they are not enough.

We are not here to bring back the past. We are here to give your child the feeling of it.

Meet Tala

Tala lives in the magical Roots & Tales tree. Tala loves stories, music, art, and adventure.

Whenever children visit Roots & Tales, Tala guides them through a world of discovery. Instead of lectures, children experience culture through hands-on activities.

Every child becomes a Roots & Tales Explorer

Five Worlds. One Adventure.

Roots & Tales is designed as an interactive experience space. Children rotate through activity zones guided by Tala.

📖

Tala's Story Circle

Storytelling and imagination

Where they don't just listen to folk tales—they become part of them. They tell stories, create characters, and discover the power of imagination.

🎨

Tala's Art Grove

Painting, clay craft, creative making

No screens. No instructions. Just hands, color, and imagination. Children work with their hands to make something real they can take home.

🥁

Tala's Rhythm Garden

Music, singing and dance

Music experienced with the whole body. Clapping, singing, moving, and feeling the rhythm of something ancient and alive.

🪁

Tala's Play Yard

Traditional games & team play

Gilli-Danda, Lagori, Kho-Kho. Games your parents played. Games that don't need a controller or WiFi.

🌱

Tala's Nature Patch

Planting, nature crafts & discovery

Reconnecting with the earth. Planting seeds, observing nature, and finding wonder in the world outside the apartment window.

A Note from the Founder

How It Works

A seamless, stress-free process for parents. A magical, unforgettable day for your child.

1

Secure a Spot

Book a weekend slot in Hyderabad. Groups are kept small to ensure every child gets personal attention.

2

Enter Tala's World

Drop your child off at our immersive venue. They’ll be welcomed by our facilitators and introduced to Tala's magical universe.

3

The 5-Zone Journey

Children rotate through our 5 interactive zones in small groups. Each activity lasts an engaging 20-25 minutes.

4

Take the Magic Home

Pick up a child who is full of stories, carrying handmade crafts, and eager to show you the new games they learned.
